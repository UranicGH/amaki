Our Process

About Amaki Arsenal

We employ a meticulous process of test after test in choosing the appropriate materials for each part of each project. We consider durability, accuracy, appearance, and the tactile feel to immerse you within your character while you cosplay.

Parts made from scratch are 3D printed from high-quality PLA filament and finished to a smooth surface without a single visible layer line. We sand and fill repeatedly to make this happen. We also purchase extra parts from trusted sources when we cannot manufacture a part in-house, such as steel mesh and helmet visors.

We spray paint all of our products for a flat finish, with minimal touch ups and realistic weathering done with careful attention to detail by hand. Our products are then covered in a clear coat lacquer to ensure the paint is protected from the elements, so you can have peace of mind from any potential damages at conventions or events.
